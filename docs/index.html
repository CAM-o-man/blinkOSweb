Why blinkOS?

            We here at hackSugar care about privacy. We know you do too. It takes effort to ensure your information remains
            private, so we've taken that effort for you.
    
            blinkOS is a privacy focused operating system based on LineageOS and Android. It ensures that and apps you download
            don't have access to you or your information, while at the same time preventing hackers from attacking your
            device through VPN hopping, a never-before-used technology, and onion routing.
